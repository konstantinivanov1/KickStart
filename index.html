Advantages of expanding your business to Bulgaria

Employees
The workforce in Bulgaria offers access to well-educated and skilled professionals.
Attractive Compensation
The minimum monthly salary in Bulgaria stands at approximately 400 euros.
Lowest Taxes in Europe
Bulgaria provides some of the lowest tax rates in Europe, including a 10% corporate income tax and a 5% dividend tax.
Flexibility in Employment Termination
Bulgaria's employment termination laws are notably more adaptable compared to those of various other European nations.
Instant Competitiveness
You instantly become competitive thanks to low overhead costs and personal income tax rates.
Minimal Initial Capital
You only require a minimal starting capital of one euro to launch your company.
Negligible Time Difference
Bulgaria boasts a minimal time difference, ensuring smooth business operations with partners across various time zones.
Direct Flight Connectivity
Bulgaria offers convenient access to various international destinations through direct flights.
High English Proficiency
Bulgarians have a strong command of the English language, ensuring effective communication in international business interactions.
